About Our College

Vaagdevi junior and Degree college was established in 2005 It's known for being the first college in our Boath constituency. The college creation was driven by the motive of need for higher education in Boath constituency. High standard education was very unique and important for rural people to make their desires happen,accessibility and connection to the local culture. The college has made significant contributions to the academics and educational development of the region and the district. Our Students have been settled in many professional fields like Engineers,Defence , Arts, Music, Commerce, Orientation, Lecturers, Teachers and excelling in various fields. The faculty has been serving our college for very long time and their Knowledge understanding of children mentality isincredible. Faculty are friendly yet make children interactive make them confident and let them know all spheres of their lives, motivational classes along with digital classes makes child confident enough to crack any exam. Vaagdevi junior and degree college establishment has changed lives of many people, We have 2 acres ground 3 acres along with the building which is very special for our infrastructure. We have Served many students and the whole area of ours and now we want expand wings and Serve you in defence sector too. We are very confident that the coaching provided will definitely give students a bright future. .
