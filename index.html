About Our College

Vaagdevi Junior & Degree College was established in 2005 and is known for being the first college in the Boath constituency. Our mission is to make higher education accessible to rural students while staying connected to local culture and values. Our students have gone on to careers in engineering, defence, arts, commerce, teaching and more.

Our faculty are experienced and approachable — we combine motivational classes with digital learning to help students succeed in exams and careers. Our campus includes academic buildings and 2–3 acres of grounds supporting sports and practical training.
