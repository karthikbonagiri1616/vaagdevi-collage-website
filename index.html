About Our College

Vaagdevi Junior & Degree College was established in 2005 — the first college in the Boath constituency. Our mission is to make high-quality higher education accessible to rural students, while staying connected to local culture and values. Our alumni excel in Engineering, Defence, Arts, Commerce, Teaching and more.

Our long-serving faculty understand student mindsets and create an interactive, motivating atmosphere. Digital and motivational classes help students build confidence to crack competitive exams. Our infrastructure includes a 2-acre ground and 3 acres with academic buildings to support comprehensive learning.
